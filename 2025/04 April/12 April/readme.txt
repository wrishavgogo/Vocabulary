Word : Contrivance



Has two meanings

1. Hack :- A device or plan that is cleverly designed for a specific purpose, or 
2. Forced mechanism  :- Something that feels forced or artificial, especially in storytelling or situations.


Usages in a sentence 

 Example 1 – As a Clever Invention:
He fixed the broken door with a bizarre contrivance made of string, magnets, and paperclips.

👉 It’s like a makeshift gadget or clever hack — maybe a bit odd, but it works!

🎭 Example 2 – In Storytelling (Artificial):
The sudden reunion of the long-lost twins felt like a contrivance to force a happy ending.

👉 Here, “contrivance” means it didn’t feel natural — like the writer just added it to wrap up the plot.
